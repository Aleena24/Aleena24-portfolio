Using Generative Adversarial Networks for Cloud Removal in Satellite Imagery

Satellite imaging for applications like agriculture, urban planning, and environmental monitoring is often hindered by cloud cover; this article presents a novel GAN-based method for cloud removal, utilizing a ResNet discriminator, UNet generator, and Perlin noise for training, achieving improved performance verified through PSNR scores using EuroSat data.

Real Image Super resolution using Generative Adversial Network

SRGAN is a novel GAN-based approach that generates high-quality, realistic images from low-resolution inputs by learning real-world degradation patterns. It outperforms existing methods in terms of visual quality and quantitative metrics, making it a valuable tool for various applications, including medical imaging and remote sensing.

Virtual Reality for Interior Design Presentation

Interior Design Presentation in Virtual Reality is a platform where interior designers can develop and share VR presentations of their work, offering benefits like time and cost savings by eliminating physical models, gaining early client feedback to avoid costly changes, and allowing clients to visualize designs in their own spaces, make informed decisions, and engage more actively in the design process.
